About Us

Welcome to the Heart of India - Madhya Pradesh!

At MP Tourism, we take immense pride in presenting the cultural kaleidoscope and natural splendors that define the soul of this magnificent state. Nestled in the heart of the Indian subcontinent, Madhya Pradesh is a tapestry of history, heritage, and breathtaking landscapes.

Our Heritage

A Treasure Trove of History: Explore the ancient legacies that have shaped the course of Indian history. From the UNESCO World Heritage Sites of Khajuraho and Sanchi to the regal forts of Gwalior and Mandu, Madhya Pradesh is a living museum that unfolds the stories of dynasties, warriors, and saints.

Our Culture

Vibrant Traditions: Immerse yourself in the vibrant tapestry of Madhya Pradesh's diverse cultures. From the lively folk dances of Bundelkhand to the melodious tunes of Malwa, every corner resonates with the rhythm of tradition. Witness the grandeur of festivals like Khajuraho Dance Festival and Orchha Utsav, where the spirit of celebration knows no bounds.

Our Nature

Land of Serenity: Discover the unspoiled beauty of Madhya Pradesh's natural wonders. Embark on a wildlife safari in the lush jungles of Kanha and Bandhavgarh National Parks, home to the majestic Bengal tigers. Cruise along the Narmada River to witness the marble rocks of Bhedaghat or traverse through the scenic landscapes of Pachmarhi, the Queen of Satpura.

Our Hospitality

Warmth of the Heartland: At MP Tourism, we believe in the true essence of Indian hospitality. Our hotels and resorts are designed to offer comfort and a touch of local flavor, ensuring that every traveler feels at home while experiencing the warmth of Madhya Pradesh.

Our Vision

Connecting Hearts, Creating Memories: Our vision is to connect hearts through unforgettable experiences. Whether you seek adventure, spirituality, or a leisurely retreat, Madhya Pradesh offers a canvas of experiences waiting to be painted with your memories.

Join us in unraveling the mysteries, embracing the traditions, and exploring the wonders of Madhya Pradesh - where every moment is a celebration of life.

Come, let Madhya Pradesh captivate your soul.
