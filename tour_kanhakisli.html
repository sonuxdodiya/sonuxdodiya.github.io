Kanha Kisli

Kanha National Park

Kanha National Park, a pristine wilderness in Madhya Pradesh, is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts. Home to the majestic Bengal tiger and diverse flora and fauna, the park offers thrilling safaris to explore its rich biodiversity.

Bamni Dadar

Bamni Dadar, also known as Sunset Point, provides breathtaking views of the sunset over the expanses of Kanha. The elevated plateau offers a serene setting for wildlife observation and is a popular spot for photography.

Kanha Museum

The Kanha Museum, situated near the park's entrance, is a repository of information on the park's wildlife, flora, and conservation efforts. Visitors can gain insights into the rich ecology and efforts to protect the region's natural treasures.

Sarv Dharm Stupa

The Sarv Dharm Stupa, a symbol of harmony, stands tall near Kanha's entrance. Surrounded by lush greenery, this stupa is a peaceful monument representing unity among different religions.

Shravan Tal

Shravan Tal, a picturesque lake within the national park, is a tranquil spot for bird watching and nature appreciation. The serene surroundings and reflection of the surrounding forests make it a peaceful retreat.

Kanha Meadows

The vast Kanha Meadows, interspersed with Sal trees, provide a scenic backdrop for wildlife encounters. Safari excursions through these meadows offer opportunities to witness diverse animal species and the park's natural beauty.
