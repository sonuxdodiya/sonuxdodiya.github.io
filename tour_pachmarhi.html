Pachmarhi

Dhupgarh

Pachmarhi, a serene hill station, is adorned with Dhupgarh, the highest point in the Satpura Range. The breathtaking panoramic views from Dhupgarh, especially during sunrise and sunset, make it a must-visit destination for nature lovers and photographers.

Apsara Vihar

Apsara Vihar, also known as Fairy Pool, is a tranquil waterfall surrounded by lush greenery. The natural pool at the base of the waterfall offers a refreshing spot for visitors to relax and enjoy the pristine beauty of Pachmarhi.

Jata Shankar Caves

Jata Shankar Caves, a sacred site, holds immense religious significance. The cave formations resemble Lord Shiva's matted locks (Jata), creating a serene atmosphere for meditation and reflection.

Pandava Caves

Pandava Caves, believed to have been the abode of the Pandavas during their exile, are a series of rock-cut caves. The intricate carvings and ancient inscriptions make these caves a fascinating archaeological site.

Handi Khoh

Handi Khoh, a dramatic gorge surrounded by steep cliffs, offers spectacular views of the Pachmarhi landscape. The horseshoe-shaped valley and dense forest create a picturesque setting for trekking and exploration.

Jheel Mahadev

Jheel Mahadev, a serene lake surrounded by hills, provides a peaceful retreat. The reflection of the surrounding landscape on the tranquil waters makes it a popular spot for nature enthusiasts and those seeking tranquility.
