Indore

Rajwada Palace

Indore, a city of historical significance, boasts the majestic Rajwada Palace. This architectural marvel, with a blend of Maratha, Mughal, and French styles, stands as a symbol of the city's royal heritage and is a must-visit for history enthusiasts.

Sarafa Bazaar

Indore's culinary landscape comes alive at night in the famous Sarafa Bazaar. A food lover's paradise, this bustling market offers a wide array of street food, snacks, and sweets, creating a vibrant and flavorful experience for visitors.

Kanch Mandir

Known as the "Glass Temple," Kanch Mandir is a Jain temple adorned with intricate glasswork. The temple's exquisite craftsmanship and serene ambiance make it a tranquil retreat for those seeking spiritual and aesthetic fulfillment.

Lal Bagh Palace

Lal Bagh Palace, once the residence of the Holkar rulers, stands as a testimony to Indore's regal past. The palace, with its opulent architecture, sprawling gardens, and vintage car collection, provides a glimpse into the city's royal history.

Patalpani Waterfall

Nature lovers can escape to Patalpani Waterfall, a scenic spot near Indore. Surrounded by lush greenery, the waterfall offers a refreshing retreat, making it a popular destination for picnics and hiking enthusiasts.

Central Museum

The Central Museum in Indore is a treasure trove of artifacts, showcasing the region's rich cultural and historical heritage. From sculptures to manuscripts, the museum provides a comprehensive insight into Madhya Pradesh's diverse history.

Khajrana Ganesh Temple

The Khajrana Ganesh Temple, dedicated to Lord Ganesha, is a revered pilgrimage site in Indore. Devotees flock to seek blessings, and the temple's vibrant festivities during Ganesh Chaturthi add to the spiritual vibrancy of the city.

Ralamandal Wildlife Sanctuary

Nature enthusiasts can explore the Ralamandal Wildlife Sanctuary on the outskirts of Indore. Home to diverse flora and fauna, the sanctuary offers a serene environment for bird watching, trekking, and reconnecting with nature.
